About

The Baseball Stat Predictor is an app that predicts hitting and pitching stats for any player in the MLB. The Baseball Stat Predictor also has a mode where any baseball player can manually enter in their past game stats and the Baseball Stat Predictor will predict their next game stats. There are 2 competition modes, one where users can predict the outcomes of future mlb matchups and another that allows users to predict game stats for any player in the MLB.
